# Workout Tracker

A comprehensive workout tracking application designed to help you plan, track, and analyze your fitness journey. Built with a modern web interface and powerful analytics to monitor your progress over time.

## What It Does

Workout Tracker is your all-in-one fitness companion that helps you organize your training, track your performance, and visualize your progress. Whether you're lifting weights, running, or cycling, this app provides the tools you need to stay on track and reach your goals.

## Key Features

### Workout Planning
- **Custom Workout Plans**: Create personalized workout templates with your favorite exercises
- **Exercise Library**: Browse and manage a comprehensive library of exercises
- **Exercise Versions**: Track different training styles for the same exercise (strength, hypertrophy, endurance)
- **Flexible Sets & Reps**: Plan workouts with customizable sets, reps, and target weights

### Active Workout Tracking
- **Live Session Tracking**: Track your sets, reps, and weights in real-time during workouts
- **Performance Metrics**: Record RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) and notes for each set
- **Timer Support**: Built-in timers for rest periods, AMRAP (As Many Reps As Possible), and EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute) workouts
- **Bodyweight Exercise Support**: Track bodyweight movements without worrying about weight inputs

### Progress & Analytics
- **Strength Progress Charts**: Visualize your strength gains over time with detailed charts
- **Personal Records**: Track your PRs and see when you hit new milestones
- **Workout History**: Browse your complete workout history with date range filtering
- **Volume Tracking**: Monitor total training volume and workout frequency

### Cardio & Garmin Integration
- **Activity Upload**: Import your cardio workouts from Garmin devices (TCX, GPX, FIT files)
- **Comprehensive Metrics**: View heart rate, pace, cadence, power, and altitude data
- **GPS Visualization**: See your running or cycling routes on an interactive map
- **Running Dynamics**: Analyze advanced metrics like ground contact time, vertical oscillation, and stride length

### User Experience
- **Dark Mode**: Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Secure Authentication**: Your data is protected with Firebase authentication
- **Smart Caching**: Fast performance with intelligent data caching
